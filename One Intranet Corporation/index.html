About Us

Welcome to One Intranet Corporation

One Intranet Corporation (OIC) is a licensed internet service provider offering wired and wireless internet services.
Partnered with Bicol One Broadband Services (BOBSI) Inc.,
which operates its own fiber optic network with a total estimated length of 1004.17 km,
stretching from San Jose Del Monte, Bulacan, to Bulan, Sorsogon.

OIC is committed to provide affordable internet connections to underserved communities,
particularly in rural areas (far flung barangays) within the Bicol Region, Rizal, and parts of Manila.
With over 20 years of expertise in design, engineering, implementation, and management of outside plant and network technical operations,
the corporation now offers end-to-end fiber optic connections to residentials and various offices.
